Taking Financial Services to the Last Mile

We are providing the digital infrastructure to store & move cash forAfrica's financially underserved population. Become an agent.

Bankly MFB is a fully licensed Digital Bank with products and services for financial institutions, retail merchants and individuals.

Our vision is to enable financial inclusion and create access by building the digital rails to connect and provide affordable financial products and services to over 48 million adult Nigerians.

At Bankly, we believe money moves faster when it’s digital. Anchored on this, we are providing a hybrid infrastructure to store and move cash easily and securely.
